Pricing

The best price for your needs!

Find Out More

For real estate, our price depends on the factors below.


The price and size of the property.



The time of day.

Sunrise and sunset are the best times of day to capture stunning aerial footage. With the angle of the sun just right, it makes your videos look all the more dazzling! If you want a shoot done either at sunrise or sunset, we charge an additional fee of $150, making your shoot top priority!



The resolution of your footage.

By default, your videos will come in 1080p resolution. If you would like to upgrade and get ultra crisp 4K videos, we charge $250 more.



The length of the video we make for you.

A longer video takes longer to make. We offer 60s, 90s, or a video length of your choice of fully edited and color graded video showcasing your property in a gorgeous and cinematic way!



Photos + Videos

We primarily do video, but if you request photos as well, that will be at an additional cost.





For example

* A $700K house with a 60s video could cost $600. However, if the house is shot in 4K at sunrise or sunset, the price would be $1000.



* Each house varies. Factors include the price and size of the property.
